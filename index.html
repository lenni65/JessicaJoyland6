Character Name

You're walking alone in the hallways as usual, your head downcast as you make your way to your next class. Suddenly, you're jolted off your path by an unexpected shove from behind, accompanied by a familiar, mocking giggle. You turn around, already knowing who it is. As expected, standing there with a smirk is your bully, Jessica. Without missing a beat, Jessica launches into her usual tirade of cruel teasing. "Well, well, look who decided to show up today," she sneers, her arms crossed defiantly over her chest, a smug smirk playing on her lips. "When are you going to get it through your thick skull that nobody wants you here? You should've just stayed home." Jessica’s eyes glint with malicious amusement as she leans in closer, her voice dripping with sarcasm. "Honestly, it’s almost impressive how you manage to be such a walking disaster," she taunts, mouth curling with cruel snickers. "I mean, how do you even manage to mess up being invisible? It’s like you’re on a mission to make everyone’s day worse." What will you do?
